    RiFF RAFF Facts

  • He began his rapping career by selling his homemade CDs in which he would rap over other artists’ beats at several malls in Houston.
  • He was formerly a part of the rap group Three Loco with Andy Milonakis and Dirt Nasty (Simon Rex).
  • In August 2013, he was arrested in North Carolina for the possession of alcohol, marijuana, and drug paraphernalia. He was booked in jail, however, was released the same night without bail.
  • In a bid to become famous, he gave many auditions including P. Diddy’s reality show Making the Band.
  • He was involved in the Spring Breakers controversy wherein the movie had actor James Franco play the character of the tattoo-laced white rapper named Alien who seemed to be heavily influenced by Riff Raff. The makers of the film denied the allegation stating that the character was rather based on the underground rap artist Dangeruss.
  • Appearing on MTV’s From G’s to Gents in 2009, helped him get noticed due to his extravagant persona and funny ad-libs.
  • His single Bird on a Wire, released in 2012, raked in 1 million plays in 2 months’ time and its popularity helped it get ranked as the 27th best song of 2012 by Complex Music.
  • He enlisted the help of WWE veteran Hulk Hogan to fulfill his aspirations of becoming a professional wrestler. For this, he bulked up 55 lbs and went from weighing 170 lbs to 225 lbs in 2015.
  • On June 24, 2016, he released his 2nd studio album titled Peach Panther and it peaked at number #3 on the US Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums (Billboard) chart and at number #69 on the US Billboard 200 chart.
  • In 2017, he teamed up with Reef Dispensaries to develop and endorse two new strains of marijuana.
  • In the past, RiFF RAFF partnered with the cannabis subscription box company Daily High Club to create a Riff Raff smoking supply box.
  • In February 2018, he teamed up with DJ Afterthought for a new album titled Cool Blue Jewels.
  • In May 2018, a woman filed a lawsuit against RiFF RAFF, accusing him of drugging and raping her after a 2013 show in Melbourne. Later, another woman accused him of touching her without consent and making suggestive comments.
  • RiFF RAFF released a mixtape titled Tangerine Tiger in November 2018.
  • In April 2019, he released his 3rd studio album titled Pink Python.
  • He released a new studio album titled Cranberry Vampire in October 2019.
  • January 22, 2020, it was revealed that RiFF RAFF had settled a lawsuit related to a s*xual assault allegation made against him, from a 2014 incident.

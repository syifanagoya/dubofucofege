Luke Hemsworth was born in Melbourne, Australia, to Leonie and Craig Hemsworth. His mother is an English teacher and his father works in social services. He comes from a family of actors, with his younger brothers Chris and Liam also pursuing successful careers in the industry. Hemsworth has a diverse heritage, with Dutch, English, Irish, Scottish, and German ancestry. He has been married to Samantha Hemsworth since 2007 and they have three daughters.

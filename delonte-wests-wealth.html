Delonte West: A Brief Look

Born on 26 July 1983, in Washington, D.C., US, Delonte West is an African- American and Native-American descent professional basketball player. His college basketball stint at Saint Joseph’s University also contributed significantly to his current net worth.

Despite losing, West received several honors and awards for his outstanding performance. He continued his basketball journey at Saint Joseph’s University, where he played alongside Jameer Nelson, contributing to the team’s remarkable season record and an Elite Eight NCAA Tournament appearance.

Entering the 2004 NBA Draft after his junior year at Saint Joseph’s, he was chosen as the 24th overall pick by the Boston Celtics.

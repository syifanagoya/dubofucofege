Ivan Menchell

Publish date: 2024-02-14

Birth Name: Ivan David Menchell

Place of Birth: New York, U.S.

Date of Birth: March 12, 1961

Ethnicity:
*Ashkenazi Jewish (father)
*English (mother)

Ivan Menchell is an American playwright, producer, and writer.

Ivan is the son of Vicky Stuart/Stewart and Louis “Lou” Menchell/Menchel. Ivan’s father was an American comedian, and was Jewish. Ivan’s mother is a singer, and is from England.

Ivan’s paternal grandparents were Isadore Menchel and Mary. Isadore was born in Austria. Mary was from Russia.

About

Finnish Swimmer who has represented her country at 2008, 2012, and 2016 Olympic Games. At the 2016 European Championships, she earned a bronze medal as a member of the Finnish 4x100 meter relay team.

Before Fame

She made her Olympic debut in 2008 at age 15. She participated in her first European Championships in 2010 at age 18, and earned the bronze medal.

Trivia

She has held various Finnish swimming records, including times for the 50 meter and 100 meter butterfly.

Family Life

She married race car driver Valtteri Bottas in September 2016.

Associated With

She competed against Kelsei Worrell and the 1st place American 4x100 meter relay swim team at the 2016 Summer Olympics.

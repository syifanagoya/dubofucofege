Introduction

Gabriella Annalisa is an American social media personality.

She is best known for her activity on the short-form video hosting service, Tik Tok.

The Origin Story

Annalisa was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, on March 13th, 2001.

Her family is from Argentina and Italy.

She went to school in Philadelphia, though precisely which school is unknown.

As a child, Annalisa had a passion for performance.

Annalisa was involved in cheerleading, singing, and dancing, and also expressed an interest in art and travel.

From a young age, she was also very interested in social media and YouTube.

Following her graduation from high school, in May of 2019, Annalisa started her own Tik Tok account.

She stated that it was her intention to begin with TikTok, and to later move on to YouTube.

TikTok Success and YouTube Career

Annalisa quickly took TikTok by storm, accruing new followers at a dizzying rate.

At the time of writing, Annalisa had an astonishing 3.6 million followers on the micro-video site, with some of her videos garnering as many as 6.9 million views.

Annalisa’s videos tend to feature the star dancing and lip-synching, and her real voice is rarely heard in her videos.

Gabriella sometimes features guest stars in her videos, but in general, she is the only person who appears.

Annalisa also maintains a YouTube account. Although it is not as successful as her TikTok account, she nevertheless has 100,000 followers on the popular video hosting website.

Her YouTube content tends to be a little different in comparison to her Tik Tok videos, where lip-synching and dancing abound; on her YouTube channel, Annalisa tends to share “slice-of-life” videos where she gives some insight into her daily life.

Such videos include details of her workout routine, her daily meals, her relationship with her boyfriend, her vacations, and various challenges.

Though these videos are not as popular as her Tik Tok output, they nevertheless pull in views well into the 15-20k range. Some of her most popular videos have more than 50,000 views.

Personal Life

Annalisa credits her favorite book as being The Great Gatsby – a book that she re-reads with some regularity.

She still enjoys dancing in her personal life, and attributes this to her interest in dancing in school, along with her time as a professional cheerleader.

Annalisa is an avid music fan. She says that her favorite genres of music are EDM (electronic dance music) and pop; she does not, however, have a favorite artist in particular.

Annalisa has a boyfriend who appears in several of her YouTube videos. She does not often refer to him by name; nor is it known how long the two of them have been dating.

Gabriella sometimes features her parents in her videos, so it can be surmised that she has a close relationship with them. However, beyond occasional appearances in her videos, little is known of her relationship with them, or if they continue to live close to one another.

Annalisa loves to travel; one of her favorite destinations is Miami, where she moved to in 2021. Society Las Vegas, a Clubhouse Media-owned content residence, was where Gabriella had previously resided.

“I feel like Miami is the new spot, like everyone is coming here,” Annalisa said in an interview with Tyler Boronski in August 2021.

“It was awesome,” Gabriella explained when talking about living in the content house. “It got me more immersed into the social media … it add opportunities for me. I really did like it.”

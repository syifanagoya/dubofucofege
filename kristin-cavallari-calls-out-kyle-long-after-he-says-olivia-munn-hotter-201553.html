Kristin Cavallari Calls Out Kyle Long, He Says Olivia Munn Is Hotter

Publish date: 2024-05-19

This WAG is wagging her finger no! Kristin Cavallari called out her husband’s teammate Kyle Long via Twitter on Wednesday, Mar. 4, after the NFL player implied that Olivia Munn was hotter.

Long, 26, hosted an informal Twitter Q&A after fans who were worried that the Chicago Bears guard and Jay Cutler‘s teammate would be traded to the Philadelphia Eagles. During the chat, one person asked the burning question on every football fan’s mind.

.@Ky1eLong who has the hotter girlfriend — Cutler or Aaron Rodgers?

— Joe Ward (@JayDubWard) March 4, 2015

As exclusively revealed by Us Weekly, Munn, 34, has been dating Green Bay Packers quarterback Aaron Rodgers, 31, since last May. (It’s also worth noting that the Bears and Packers share an unprecedented midwestern rivalry with both teams facing off against each other in the NFC North division.)

“@JayDubWard: .@Ky1eLong who has the hotter girlfriend — Cutler or Aaron Rodgers?” Well Cutty is married.. And I love the newsroom.. Sooo

— Kyle (@Ky1eLong) March 4, 2015

Cavallari then caught wind of his comments and was less-than-pleased with her hubby’s pal.

@Ky1eLong @JayDubWard ummmm excuse me

— Kristin Cavallari (@KristinCav) March 4, 2015

The two, however, appear to share a friendly relationship — with the Laguna Beach alum even knowing Long’s “type” of girl.

“@KristinCav: @Ky1eLong @JayDubWard ummmm excuse me” let’s fight about it KC u know I’m into brunettes

— Kyle (@Ky1eLong) March 4, 2015

@Ky1eLong natural brunette right here ????

— Kristin Cavallari (@KristinCav) March 4, 2015

Following their exchange, Cavallari posted a stunning selfie ahead of a night out with her husband. “Date night,” the shoe designer captioned the pic.

While Long’s future still remains uncertain, Cavallari and Cutler will be with the Bears for quite some time. The Bears signed Cutler to a seven-year contract last January, with a guaranteed total of $54 million in the first three years.

The California-raised reality star, luckily, enjoys watching her husband play. “I get very into it,” Cavallari told a local Chicago news station in 2013. “I’m at every game if I’m in town.”

Munn echoed Cavallari’s enthusiasm for football this past January with Seth Meyers. “Every home game, I am there,” the Newsroom actress said. “I think I’m a good fan. I’m there and I’m screaming a lot. I think it’s really important to scream and my acupuncturist told me energy follows thought. That was a very Hollywood sentence for you guys.”
